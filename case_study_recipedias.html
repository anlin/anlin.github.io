Recipedias

Role

UX Designer

Project Goal

Design Recipedia app allows users to easily under represented recipes and buy ingredients for it.

Target audience

Users, who live in a big city, wants to cook their hometown dishes and buy ingredients online.

Key challenges or constraints

Users cannot find recipes online and cannot buy ingredients in the place they live.

Research study details

A primary user group identified through research was working adults and students who cannot find their respective recipes from hometown. Other user problems is finding difficult to get ingredients for cooking.

Initial design concepts

Create an app to let users search recipe and buy its ingredients online.
