Roomdom

Role

UX Designer

Project Goal

Design a responsive website for users who need to find apartments without paying agent fees online.

Target audience

Users who is looking for apartments onine and don't want to pay for the agent fees.

Key challenges or constraints

Users couldn't find apartments online. They must go around and find the contact.

Research study details

A primary user group identified through research was working adults and university/college students who cannot find their apartments online. Other user problems is they do not wish to pay agent fees.

Initial design concepts

Create a responsive website to let users search apartments and contact owner online without going through agents.
